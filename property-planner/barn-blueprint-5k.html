🎯 Design Highlights

  • Court + Ninja combo: Ninja obstacles mount on walls/ceiling around court perimeter — same floor space, double function
  • Expanded Gym (660 sq ft): Dedicated weight room with power rack, cables, dumbbells, bench, and cardio zone
  • Separate Recovery Zone: Cold plunge + red light therapy with direct gym access — outdoor sauna on deck
  • Massive Bar Lounge (841 sq ft): Full bar, 85" TV, sectional, poker table — overlooks the court
  • Full mezzanine utilization: No wasted space — all 2,000 sq ft enclosed with bathroom + storage
  • Clear stair access: Central stairs connect both floors — marked on both blueprints
