🎯 Space-Saving Design Moves

  • Court + Ninja combo: Ninja obstacles mount on walls/ceiling around court perimeter — same floor space, double function
  • Bouldering → Mezzanine: Climb the wall to reach the entertainment level (fun entry point)
  • Games + Stage merged: Drum kit in corner, pool table center — they don't conflict
  • Gym + Recovery combo: 750 sq ft with cold plunge & red light built in — sauna goes outside
  • Golf sim (500 sf): Compact but full swing room with 12' ceiling clearance
  • 40% mezzanine coverage: Maximizes usable space while keeping court open and airy
